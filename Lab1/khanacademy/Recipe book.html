Classic Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 15 minutes prep, 10 minutes cook
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza Dough 1 ball (approx 250g)
Tomato Sauce 1/2 cup
Fresh Mozzarella 125g
Fresh Basil A few leaves

Step 1: Preheat your oven to its highest setting (usually 250°C/500°F).

Step 2: Roll out the dough on a floured surface until thin.

Step 3: Spread the tomato sauce evenly, leaving a small border for the crust.

Step 4: Tear the mozzarella into chunks and place on top.

Step 5: Bake for 8-10 minutes until the crust is charred and cheese is bubbly. Add fresh basil leaves immediately after removing from the oven.

Simple Avocado Toast

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Whole Grain Bread 2 slices
Ripe Avocado 1 whole
Red Pepper Flakes 1 pinch
Lemon Juice 1 teaspoon

Step 1: Toast your bread slices until golden brown and crispy.

Step 2: In a small bowl, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

Step 3: Spread the mixture generously over the toast.

Step 4: Sprinkle with red pepper flakes and a drizzle of olive oil if desired.
